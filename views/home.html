Frequently Asked Questions

What is ScoreSync?
ScoreSync is your premium sports hub designed to provide live global standings, deep team performance analytics, and a seamless ticket booking experience.
How do I book a ticket?
Simply navigate to the "Tickets" tab, search for your favorite event, choose your preferred seats on our interactive map, and complete the secure payment.
Are the player ratings official?
Our ratings (1.0 to 10.0) are calculated using advanced statistical models based on real-time match performance and historical consistency.
What payment methods do you support?
We support highly secure transactions via UPI, Credit/Debit cards, and major Net Banking providers to ensure your booking is safe.
Can I manage my bookings?
Yes, once you are logged in, you can view your entire purchase history and download digital receipts directly from your "My Profile" page.
How does the "Favorite Sports" feature work?
By marking sports as favorites in your profile, our system tailor-fits real-time alerts and trending events specifically to your interests.
What sports categories are covered?
We offer comprehensive data for Football (major European leagues), Basketball (NBA/EuroLeague), Cricket, Tennis, Formula 1, MMA, and more.
Is ScoreSync available on mobile?
Absolutely. ScoreSync is built with a fully responsive mobile-first architecture, ensuring a premium experience on any device or screen size.
How secure is my account?
We utilize industry-standard encryption for passwords and offer account recovery options via mobile number to keep your data protected.
What happens if a match is cancelled?
In the event of a cancellation, you will receive a real-time update. Refund processing is handled automatically based on the event provider's specific policy.
